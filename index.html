Synopsis

False Summits follows Zara’s journey to retrace the final expedition of her husband, Raja Azlan Shah, and to confront the unanswered questions surrounding his death on Gunung Tahan.

Through witness accounts, official records, medical evidence, and route reconstruction, the film examines preparedness, duty of care, and the systems that failed to provide clarity when it mattered most.

This archive is presented as a documentary research resource for False Summits. It distinguishes between verified documents, witness recollections, unresolved discrepancies, and editorial analysis. Sensitive personal information may be redacted where necessary.
